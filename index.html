About

IFaaS stands for “Integer Factorization as a Service,” which is essentially, a platform to get factorization of numbers and their associated work (setup, electricity, hardware, etc.) done via providing financial incentive for people with the know-how to do so. The financial incentive is needed, as most people nowadays don’t have a surplus of money nor most other resources; hence, they cannot afford to run such computations, and use a bunch of electricity, without getting more than enough made to cover their explicit and implicit costs, even if they are enthusiastic about such endeavors. Providing incentive may also attract more interest towards this field, and in turn, spur development of a more integrated and efficient solution for factorization as a whole
